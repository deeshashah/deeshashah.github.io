Autoshorts

There are many news websites that provide a lot of infomation to the readers that distracts them from reading their article of interest. Thus, studying various news websites and using design principles of materialise, we developed Autoshorts. Autoshorts is a news reading application where users can view latest news articles of various categories. Before viewing any article, a user would be able to view its summary which is automatically generated by our extractive summarisation algorithm

Studeely

During my undergraduate, I had seen my teachers spending alot of time setting quizzes. I thought it would be great if the process was automated. I used a corpus of around 1000 Wikipedia articles consisting of Physics topics, trained a model which could automatically produce fill in the blank questions as well as puzzling distractors. I was able to do it using concepts of natural language processing.
